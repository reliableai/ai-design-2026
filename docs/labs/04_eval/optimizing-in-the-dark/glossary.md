# Glossary

Key terms as used in this series.

---

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Noise** | Random variation across repeated measurements |
| **Uncertainty** | Lack of confidence in an estimate due to noise, sampling, or bias |
| **Variability** | Genuine differences across users, contexts, or inputs |
| **Bias** | Systematic error that does not average out |
| **Variance** | Quantitative measure of spread |
